Findings and recommendations

Banking and credit cards

Action required
Findings
  • Multiple bank and credit-card accounts require reconciliation or documented cleanup.
  • Two active checking registers ending in 2534 overlap across multiple years.
  • Several credit-card accounts have never been reconciled.
Recommendations
  • Reconcile applicable accounts to the statements provided.
  • Determine whether the two 2534 registers are separate accounts, a duplicate register, or split historical activity.
  • Correct duplicate, missing, uncleared, or misposted transactions where support exists.

Accounts receivable and Undeposited Funds

Action required
Findings
  • Undeposited Funds is approximately $1.5 million.
  • More than 1,200 historical customer payments require review.
  • A/R includes positive, negative, and aged customer balances.
Recommendations
  • Match customer payments to deposits where support exists.
  • Correct duplicated or improperly applied payments, credits, and refunds.
  • Document balances that require client confirmation or collection decisions.

Sales tax and payroll liabilities

Needs support
Findings
  • Historical sales-tax balances cannot be verified from QuickBooks alone.
  • Payroll-liability balances may include legacy mapping or unsupported activity.
Recommendations
  • Compare QuickBooks balances to returns, payroll reports, filings, and payment confirmations provided by BCR.
  • Correct bookkeeping entries where sufficient support exists.

Chart of Accounts, P&L, and Balance Sheet

Action required
Findings
  • The Chart of Accounts contains duplicate, generic, and incorrectly typed accounts.
  • Opening Balance Equity, owner activity, loans, and other historical balances require review.
  • Several asset and liability balances appear stale, negative, or unsupported.
Recommendations
  • Merge, rename, reclassify, or deactivate accounts where appropriate.
  • Review income and expense classifications for material inconsistencies.
  • Correct supported balances and use documented cleanup entries where full reconstruction is not reasonably possible.

Detailed Cleanup Scope

Bank and credit cards

Review registers, identify duplicate, missing, uncleared, or misposted transactions, reconcile to records provided, and document unresolved differences.

Overlapping 2534 registers

Compare dates, balances, transfers, reconciliations, and statements to determine how each register was used and make supported corrections.

A/R and Undeposited Funds

Review invoices, payments, credits, refunds, deposits, and historical balances; correct duplicated or improperly applied activity where support exists.

Sales tax and payroll

Compare QuickBooks balances with returns, filings, reports, payments, and other support supplied by the client.

Chart of Accounts and P&L

Correct material classification issues, duplicate or unnecessary accounts, incorrect account types, and inconsistent income or expense posting.

Balance Sheet

Review loans, equity, Opening Balance Equity, retained earnings-related activity, owner activity, and other unsupported or stale balances.

Client Records Required

BCR must provide complete bank and credit-card statements, documentation identifying both 2534 accounts, loan statements and amortization schedules, payroll reports and payroll-tax filings, sales-tax returns and payment confirmations, CPA workpapers or adjusting entries, merchant processor reports when applicable, and explanations for unclear owner activity or unsupported historical balances.

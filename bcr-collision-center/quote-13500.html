Detailed Cleanup Scope

Bank and credit cards

Review registers, identify duplicate, missing, uncleared, or incorrectly posted transactions, reconcile to records provided, and document unresolved differences.

Overlapping 2534 registers

Compare dates, balances, transfers, reconciliations, and statements to determine how each register was used and make supported corrections.

Accounts Receivable

Review invoices, payments, credits, refunds, and historical A/R balances; correct duplicated or improperly applied activity where support exists.

Undeposited Funds

Match customer payments to deposits where possible and resolve or document unsupported historical balances.

Sales tax

Review bookkeeping balances against returns, reports, payments, and CPA support provided by BCR.

Payroll liabilities

Review payroll-liability balances against payroll reports, filings, payment confirmations, and other support.

Chart of Accounts and P&L

Review duplicate or unnecessary accounts, incorrect account types, and material income or expense classification inconsistencies.

Loans, equity, and remaining Balance Sheet

Review loans, owner activity, Opening Balance Equity, retained earnings-related activity, and other unsupported or stale balances.

Client Records Required

BCR must provide complete bank and credit-card statements, documentation identifying both 2534 accounts, loan statements and amortization schedules, payroll reports and payroll-tax filings, sales-tax returns and payment confirmations, CPA workpapers or adjusting entries, merchant processor reports when applicable, and explanations for unclear owner activity or unsupported historical balances.

Cleanup Standard and Historical Limitations

The cleanup will use the best available records and reasonable bookkeeping methods. PRNG Bookkeeping Services is not required to recreate missing third-party documentation or prove historical activity that cannot be supported by BCR’s records.

Unsupported historical balances may be corrected using documented cleanup entries when complete reconstruction is not reasonably possible. Material unsupported items remaining after cleanup will be identified in the final documentation.

Exclusions

This is bookkeeping cleanup only. It does not include tax-return preparation or filing, amended returns, legal or tax advice, audit or attestation services, collections, payroll processing, sales-tax filing, lender verification, or work requested outside the signed scope.

## Four-price review method

For substantial cleanup quotes, establish:

1. A defensible floor
2. A defensible cap
3. Two intermediate versions
4. Identical scope across all versions so only price and payment amounts change
5. Final selection based on scope, precedent, effective rate, closing risk, and professional judgment

## Ongoing monthly bookkeeping

Working monthly fee after cleanup: approximately $850 per month.

Assumptions:

- Books begin clean.
- Monthly volume remains near 300 transactions.
- Payroll is processed elsewhere or remains automated.
- PRNG reviews payroll entries and liabilities but does not run payroll unless separately scoped.
- PRNG maintains sales tax bookkeeping but does not prepare or file returns unless separately scoped.
- Heavy invoicing, collections, inventory, or job-costing work is excluded unless added in writing.
